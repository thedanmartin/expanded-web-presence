Hello and Welcome to Dan Martin Dot Net

Here you'll find information about me and the things I do, presented in an evolving online format.

First, a little bit about me: I'm a first year student in the Technical Communication and Information Design program at Illinois Institute of Technology. For fall 2014 I'm taking two courses: COM 542, Knowledge Management and COM 530, Standards-Based Web Design (this page is a project for COM 530). I am also a teaching assistant for AAH 119, History of World Architecture, as well as the book review editorial assistant for HOPOS Journal.

I used to work for the University of Chicago Press Journals Division in a variety of production roles. My undergraduate time was spent at Michigan Technological University. I'm originally from Houghton, Michigan, but I've lived in Chicago for 10 years.

I'm interested in learning about all types of publishing technologies, open access publishing, web forums, geography, and whatever else ya got. In my spare time I like watching hockey or soccer, listening to music, riding my bike, and making/eating food. You can find me on the internet all over the place, but places that are specifically relevant to this page are my Twitter and Github pages. Sometimes you can find me IRL in 200 Siegel Hall on IIT's campus.
