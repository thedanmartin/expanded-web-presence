Journals Production Controller

I was responsible for the production of 12 academic journals (50+ issues per year total) across a variety of science and humanistic disciplines on monthly, quarterly, biannual, and annual schedules. These journals are both university-owned and professional association-owned publications. All journals are published in print, online, and e-book editions. This work required collaboration with a variety of parties, namely the authors, journal offices/managing editors, copy-editing staff, technical staff, and several different vendors. Manuscripts are entered into an in-house database once accepted by each journal and shepherded through editing, proofing, revision, typesetting, printing, and electronic presentation. Along the way I prepared electronic art for publication, kept track of the proofing and revision process, typeset and paginated individual issues, posted electronic editions including making edits to XML/HTML, performed quality control on online and e-book presentations, as well as gave final approval on hard proofs. All of my work was aided by a variety of commercial publishing software and in-house UNIX-based technology.
