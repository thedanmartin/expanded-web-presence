Single-Page Web Presence

The second project for COM 530: A single, stand-alone page about me, using a mobile-first design and responsive Web design techniques. Again, some of the writing may be familiar.

Web Form Re-Design

The third project for COM 530: A re-design of the login and registration pages from ilxor.com, a discussion forum I frequent. My re-design combines the login and registration forms into a single page, streamlines the instructions somewhat, and incorporates CSS styles augmented by the use of Formalize, among other changes.
